    David Hasselhoff – Hooked on a Feelin’

    While browsing my earlier posts, I came across this one that linked to David Hasselhoff’s masterpiece of a music video, Hooked on a Feelin’. Unfortunately, for some reason David has taken the video off of his website. Perhaps he wanted to protect his career? Anyway, I found the video on YouTube, and after all these years, it’s still a great watch:

    [flv:/blog/video/HookedOnAFeeling.mp4 480 360]

    My favorite parts:

    • David flying out of a dinner party and into the African Savanna
    • David hopping up and down with Africans
    • Stupid CG tricks involving a 3D cube
    • David flapping his arms and flying with a bunch of birds
    • David looking at the side of the boat repeatedly, checking for assassins
    • David standing on a motorcycle, and then realizing he doesn’t need one, BECAUSE HE CAN FLY!
    • Savanna David singing a duet with flying David
    • Eskimo snowboarding David catching a fish and biting into it
    • Crazy dance immediately following the fish catch

    There’s also bad lip syncing everywhere and some strange puppet thing makes an appearance a few times. Hooked on a Feelin’ has got to be one of my favorite music videos ever. I’m glad that I can once again bring this video to you, my loyal reader(s?).

    New Super Mario Brothers DS Video!

    I found this on Boing Boing. It’s a “leaked” video of the New Super Mario Brothers game for the DS.

    Looks like the level is a combination of the desert level from Mario 2 (complete with quicksand) and has those platforms from the original Mario Bros. Too bad Mario gets hit right after he gets the flower. I wanted to see him spit fire! Damn that annoying Lakitu bastard!

    Another thing: When Mario gets turned into little Mario and picks up the fire flower for the first time, guess what happens? Nope, he doesn’t turn into big Mario (that’s what would’ve happened in the original Super Mario Bros.), he turns into fire Mario! Yes, the greatest wish of my 9 year old self has come true! Damn, I’m a nerd!

    Generation of Chaos Preorders Online!

    Generation of Chaos, a strategy RPG for the PSP just went online for preorder today (err… yesterday) at NIS America. Being the NIS fanboy that I am, I’m really considering getting this. The only problem? I don’t have a PSP…

    Now, the PSP is pretty well known for not having any games that “sell the system.” If Generation of Chaos rocks, it could very well be the turning point for me.

    I’ll wait for the reviews personally. Sure, everything that Nippon Ichi does is money in the bank, but this game was actually developed by Idea Factory. That said, it might not be the SRPG I’m looking for. If only Nippon Ichi would develop for the Nintendo DS… Now that would be something!

    Elements of Modern Web Design

    I found this link from BoingBoing. It basically goes through the elements of the more modern web pages out there. By modern, I really mean shiny and pretty. There’s some really good info in here.

    The main idea is to use gradients to smooth things out and whitespace to be easy on the eyes. I think I might need to work a bit on the latter, myself.

    I’ve been trying to make the design of my blog to look somewhat cooler, like Mav Web Solutions people do. So far, the plasticy header and sidebar things are to my liking, but I think there’s probably more I could do. But first I need to get Photoshop! After that, I need some Photoshop skills…
