The Grad School Chronicles is written by Hung Truong. Hung graduated from the University of New Mexico with a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and a minor in Japanese in 2006 and the University of Michigan with a Master’s of Science in Information in 2009.

The blog as it was described, in past future tense:

The Grad School Chronicles will include regular updates on the process that Hung will use to apply to grad school. This includes picking schools to apply to, writing a personal statement, getting letters of recommendation and taking the GRE.

Once Hung has (hopefully) been accepted into a grad school, the blog will shift its focus to grad school life.
